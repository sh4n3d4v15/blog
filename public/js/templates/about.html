About me

I have been programming for around 18 months and have spent a lot of that time focusing on javaScript.

Being relatively new to coding, I knew I had a long journey to proficiency and a huge skills gap to fill. Initially, after learning html and CSS, I played a lot with PHP and got a site up and running with Codeigniter. I loved it and learnt all about OOP! I also learned that I really like MVC and it opened my eyes to coding practices.

I got into node.js about 6 months ago and an idea set in: If I focus on javaScript, I will be learning the most ubiquitous of the programming languages and also be getting in on the “node.js movement”, at the same time as a lot of more experienced programmers. Since then, I’ve got cosy with node.js, Backbone.js, AMD, TDD and all the other cool cats.

This site is me trying to share what I’m learning, and give a little back to the community that made learning all this stuff possible and accessible.
